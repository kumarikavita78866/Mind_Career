Frequently Ask Questions


What is the use of MindCareer?
MindCareer is India's #1 Professional that connects job seekers with employers. It helps candidates or job seekers to apply for jobs & talk to the corresponding HRs of the companies directly and vice versa.
How do I get job using MindCareer?
It is very simple to find jobs on MindCareer.
  1. Login in MindCareer using your phone number and complete your profile.
  2. Select the job according to your choice and apply.
  3. Call HR to schedule an interview.
How can an employers post a job on MindCareer?
Employers can post job on MindCareer by following steps:-
  1. Register with your mobile number at MindCareer.online
  2. Click on Post and enter basic details about the job.
  3. Add their requirements from the candidates and interview information.
  4. Click on finish and post.
What type of jobs are available on MindCareer?
There are different types of jobs available on MindCareer like Full-Time jobs, Part-Time jobs, Women jobs, Fresher's jobs etc.
Can user's earn money from MindCareer?
Yes, by applying the jobs available on MindCareer. It doesn't provide jobs directly but it helps candidates to apply for the jobs across the top cities in India.
